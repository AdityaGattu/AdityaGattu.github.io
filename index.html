Interactive Player Interaction Network

Explore the Dynamics: This interactive graph maps the on-field interactions between players, highlighting patterns of play. Red nodes mark frequent negative interactions, potentially indicating strategic weaknesses or Match fixing possibility. Use the filters to view by country or club and uncover insights into team and player connections.

Select Countries

Negative Interaction (-ve): Described as an event where a player's interaction with another player results in a goal for the opposing team. These could be mistakes like lost balls or poor passes leading to counter-attacks.

Negative Impression: This is a metric indicating players who have repeatedly had negative interactions with the same player, possibly suggesting match fixing between those two.

Red Node: Player with potential match-fixing risk i.e has more negative impressions
Green Node: Player with no negative impressions
Grey Node: Player exhibiting normal behavior
Red Edge: Negative Impression exists between those two players
Green Edge: No negative Impression between those two players
